# AI Image Prompts for Click & Teach Website

Use these prompts in image generators (DALL·E, Midjourney, Ideogram, etc.) to create graphics that match the site’s theme: **indigo/slate/sky palette**, **modern**, **STEM education**, **digital teaching**.

**Site palette:** Deep slate/navy (#0f172a–#1e293b), indigo primary (#4f46e5–#6366f1), sky blue (#0ea5e9), soft gradients. Style: clean, professional, education/edtech.

---

## 1. Stats Splash (above metrics)

**Purpose:** Hero-style visual above the “Click & Teach” title and metric cards.

| Field | Value |
|-------|--------|
| **Dimensions** | 1200 × 600 px (or 2:1) |
| **Aspect ratio** | 2:1 |
| **Background** | Solid or very soft gradient: dark slate (#0f172a) to deep indigo (#1e1b4b). No transparency. |
| **Format** | PNG or JPG |

**Prompt:**
> Modern flat illustration for an edtech landing page. Abstract geometric shapes suggesting growth and data: subtle upward bars, soft glowing orbs in indigo and sky blue, thin light lines connecting nodes. No people, no text. Palette: dark slate background, indigo (#4f46e5), sky blue (#0ea5e9), white/light gray accents. Minimal, premium, tech. 2:1 aspect ratio, dark mode.

Clean, professional, STEM education. Transparent or dark background. 4:3.
